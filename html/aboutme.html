ABOUT ME

Where I'm From...

Hi there, my name is Willem and welcome to my corner of the woods. I grew up on my parents farm and times were often tough, so there wasn't always money for fancy cars and fancy toys etc. We did however have fishing rods! As a farmer's son I had little time to myself to do what I want or to do the things that most kids do. I had plenty of chores and always had to carry my weight. If it wasn't school work that needs doing, it was farming work.

Time to time, however, my father gave me some time off to do anything I wanted, so I went fishing with my Grandfather who loves fishing as much as I do! Everything I know about fishing I learned from him. Fishing has tought me many things such as patience, how important it is to spend time with family and that there is no better way to learn things than by teaching yourself. With all that said, I really hope that my website will inspire and encourage you to go do a little fishing.

Where am I Now...

Okay, so, I've told you about my younger days on the farm and how hard I worked. Luckily it paid off! My Father tought me how to be a hard worker just as he was and that has stuck with me all my life. After School my father told me: "No matter what, I will pay for your studies so that you won't struggle or have any dept." and he kept true to his promise.

I studied Engineering for five long years to get my Diploma and Bachelors. Yes, I am a qualified Mechatronic Engineer! For 3 years throughout my professional career I worked as an Automation Engineer for various companies. During those three years, though, I eventually I lost my passion for Engineering and so I searched for something similar to it, but with less travelling, more family time and more me time.

One day, a friend who studied Engineering with me wanted to meet up. We talked about our careers and felt we had jobs but no passions for what we were doing, so I guess you could say that we were "in the same boat". Both of us decided on finding someting new to do and we started to do free courses on Web Development. I enjoyed it very much and so I enrolled at a Web Development bootcamp. So now I am studying Web Development and thus this Website was born!
